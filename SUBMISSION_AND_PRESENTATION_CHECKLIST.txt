## 🔍 COMMON CHALLENGES & RESPONSES

### "Your model accuracy isn't perfect (AUC 0.78)"
**Response**: "Correct. That's realistic. In deployment, we'd A/B test on 10% of drivers first to calibrate. This is forecast-level accuracy, not perfection. The point is directional clarity."

### "Why not just increase EV bonus to $60?"
**Response**: "[Point to S-curve] Diminishing returns. At $40 we get 65%. At $60 we get 82%—a 17% lift for 50% more cost. ROI drops sharply. $40 is the sweet spot."

### "What if drivers don't respond as predicted?"
**Response**: "We monitor weekly. If adoption is slower, we have two options: increase bonus $5-10 next month, or investigate adoption blockers. If faster, hold steady. It's an iterative process."

### "Competitors will copy this"
**Response**: "Possibly. But if they adopt similar policies, EV adoption accelerates globally—better for all. If they don't, we gain competitive advantage in urban markets. Either way, we benefit."

### "What about suburban drivers?"
**Response**: "Zone analysis shows suburbs respond +21-23% to Scenario B vs +25-26% in Tier-1. Still positive. We start where ROI is highest (DT), then expand. Suburbs get phased in weeks 3-6."
